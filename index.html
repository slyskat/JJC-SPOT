Our Journey: From a Small Idea to a Flavorful Chain

2016: The Seed of Passion

JJC Spot began as a small idea by Davis Lawal, fueled by his deep passion for authentic Jamaican-style grilled food. It started as a single food stand in the bustling streets of Lagos, quickly gaining a loyal following.

2018: First Brick-and-Mortar

The overwhelming success of the food stand led to our first permanent location in Lekki Phase 1, Lagos. This marked a pivotal moment, allowing us to expand our menu and reach more jerk chicken lovers.

2020-2022: Expanding Across Nigeria

Driven by growing demand and our commitment to quality, we proudly opened new branches in Yaba (Lagos), Ring Road (Ibadan), and Wuse 2 (Abuja), bringing our unique flavors to more communities.

Today: A Recognizable Chain

JJC Spot has blossomed into a beloved food chain with multiple thriving branches across Nigeria, always upholding our core values of quality, taste, and customer satisfaction. We continue to innovate while staying true to our roots.

A Message from Our Founder, Davis Lawal

"From a humble food stand, my dream was to share the authentic taste of Jamaica with everyone. JJC Spot is more than just food; it's a celebration of culture, quality, and community. Thank you for being part of our flavorful journey."
